Featured Project

My Main Personal Project Driven by Passion

Rendercast Real-Time 3d Engine

This project is a WebGL-based 3D engine consisting of two main parts: the rendering engine and the audio engine. The rendering engine handles real-time 3D graphics using WebGL and WebXR, directly in the browser.

Using advanced rendering techniques, the experience maintains high performance while offering realistic lighting, physics interactions, and responsive environment design.

The audio engine is built on entirely new methods and algorithms that I developed myself—beyond what AI can create, imitate, or even conceive! I plan to publish a research paper on this in the future and will share the link here
